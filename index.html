About me

My name is Anusha Dharmasena. I am a 31-year-old Software Engineer, originally from Sri Lanka, with more than 8 years of experience in the field. I currently live in Melbourne (yes, that's in Australia) with my family. In fact, we moved here only a few months ago!

Before I moved to Melbourne, I used to live in Japan for about two thirds of my career. When I was there, I developed printer driver related products for Canon Inc. I did not work at Canon; I worked for a company in Canon group of companies; at least, that's how they classified it. I was actively involved in developing the latest Windows V4 Printer Driver in its infant stages. I believe that Canon has finally switched to the V4 driver for their printers, entirely.

Unfortunately, the most important project in the point of view of my career, which I would call the "SVG project", cannot be discussed in detail as it was a prototype product back them. Now it probably is in production; there is no way to know as it was not targeted at the general consumer. It was my first Agile project, and I was a main designer and developer of its team, right from the beginning.

I love programming, blogging and learning new things. Currently I am developing a React.js application at work, which has inspired me to move into web development. Web development is cool! That's actually what made me want to design a website for myself.

I am married and we have two kids: a son and a daughter and they are so young.

I love playing games - especially FPS games - on my PC, however with kids, it is a bit difficult to find time. And my PC needs a bit of upgrading to play the latest games. I love (well, loved) playing cricket and still watch them on TV.
