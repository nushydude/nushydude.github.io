About me

My name is Anusha Dharmasena. I am a 31-year-old Software Engineer, originally from Sri Lanka, with more than 8 years of experience in the field. I currently live in Melbourne (yes, that's Melbourne in Australia) with my family. In fact, we moved here only a few months ago!

Before I moved to Melbourne, I used to live in Japan for about two thirds of my career. When I was there, I developed printer driver related products for Canon Inc. I did not work at Canon; I worked for a company in the Canon group of companies; at least, that's how they classified it. However, the year before leaving Japan for good, I was deployed in one of their technical centres in Kawasaki city, where I was actively involved in developing the latest Windows V4 Printer Driver in its infant stages.

Unfortunately, the most important project in the point of view of my career, which I would call the "SVG project", cannot be discussed in detail as it was a prototype product back then. Now it probably is in production; however there is no way to know as it was not targeted at the general consumer. It was my first Agile project, and I was a main designer/developer of the team, right from its inception.

I love programming, blogging and learning new things. Currently I am developing a React.js application at work, which has inspired me to move into web development. Web development is cool! That's actually what made me want to design a website for myself. I have learned a lot of things so far, and it will always be a work in progress.
